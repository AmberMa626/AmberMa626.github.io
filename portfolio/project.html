Wine Retailing Forecast using Tableau, Python

Our team is dedicated to find a best state in United States to build a wine retailing company. Considering serveral relevant factors, we found that New Hamsphire is the best result. Our processes includes:

  • Explored potential influential factor, collected and cleaned business data from 9 sources.
  • Processed raw data into independent variables with the assistant from multivariate LASSO regression result; created the average retailer turnover variable (Python, Tableau)
  • Performed the entire evaluative process, including project scoping, business stakeholder analysis, data process modeling, solution prototyping, usability testing, and final presentation delivery.
